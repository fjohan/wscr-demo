1. What kind of technology is it?

ScriptLog Web is a browser-based keystroke logging and replay system. It records detailed writing events such as typing, deletions, cursor movement, pauses, and revisions, and supports process-level playback and analysis.

2. What can it be used for?

It can be used for writing-process research, educational writing studies, revision behavior analysis, and comparisons across writers, tasks, or proficiency levels.

3. What types of questions can it answer?

It can answer questions about when and where revision happens, how pause behavior evolves, whether writing is linear or recursive, and how process patterns differ between participants or conditions.
